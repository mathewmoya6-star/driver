🧭 Module Structure

10 Comprehensive Modules covering all aspects of driver training

01
🟢 ROAD SAFETY FUNDAMENTALS
  • Principles of Safe Driving - Responsibility, Risk Perception
  • Human Error as Main Cause of Accidents (83% in Kenya)
  • Defensive Driving Concept - Anticipation, Safe Distance
  • Avoiding Aggressive Driving & Situational Awareness
  • Accident Prevention - Speeding, Alcohol, Drugs, Fatigue, Distraction
02
🔵 TRAFFIC LAWS & NTSA REGULATIONS
  • Traffic Act Cap 403 - Legal Duties, Offences, Penalties
  • Drunk Driving Laws (Section 44) - Fine Ksh 100k or imprisonment
  • NTSA Regulations - Driver Licensing System, PSV Rules
  • Vehicle Inspection Requirements & Road Safety Compliance
  • Obeying Traffic Police, Lane Discipline, Right of Way
03
🟡 ROAD SIGNS & MARKINGS
  • Regulatory Signs (Circle) - STOP, GIVE WAY, Speed Limits
  • Warning Signs (Triangle) - Danger alerts, bends, slippery road
  • Informational Signs (Rectangle) - Direction, Services, Distance
  • Road Markings - White lines (lane division), Yellow lines (no overtaking)
  • Pedestrian Crossings, Traffic Light System, Police Hand Signals
04
🟠 MOTORCYCLE (BODA BODA) SAFETY
  • Motorcycle Handling - Balance, Braking Techniques, Cornering
  • Passenger Safety - Helmet Requirements, Passenger Limits
  • Safe Mounting/Dismounting, Lane Splitting Dangers
  • Blind Spots of Vehicles, Weather Hazards (Rain, Dust)
  • Legal Requirements - Licensing, Insurance, NTSA Rules
05
🔴 PSV (PUBLIC SERVICE VEHICLE) TRAINING
  • Passenger Safety - Boarding, Alighting, Seatbelt Enforcement
  • Overloading Prevention, 8-Hour Driving Rule (Section 66A)
  • Speed Limit Enforcement, PSV Badge Requirements
  • Customer Service Standards, Conflict Handling
  • Emergency Procedures - Accident Response, Fire, Passenger Evacuation
06
🟣 VEHICLE HANDLING & CONTROL
  • Basic Vehicle Controls - Steering, Gears (Manual/Auto)
  • Acceleration and Braking Techniques
  • Uphill and Downhill Driving, Overtaking Safely
  • Parking Methods - Angle, Flush, Parallel
  • Load Distribution, Tyre Pressure, Road Grip Awareness
07
⚫ DEFENSIVE & ADVANCED DRIVING
  • Hazard Perception - Identifying Danger Early
  • Predicting Driver Behavior, Two-Second & 4-Second Rule
  • Safe Overtaking Rules, Night Driving Safety
  • MSM Technique (Mirror, Signal, Manoeuvre)
  • Adverse Conditions - Rain, Fog, Slippery Roads
08
🟤 FIRST AID & EMERGENCY RESPONSE
  • Accident Response Steps - Secure Scene, Call 999
  • Dr. A.B.C - Danger, Response, Airway, Breathing, Circulation
  • CPR Basics, Bleeding Control, Fracture Handling
  • Recovery Position, Shock Management
  • Emergency Contacts - Police, Ambulance, Fire Brigade
09
🟢 VEHICLE INSPECTION & MAINTENANCE
  • Pre-Trip Inspection - Brakes, Lights, Tyres, Engine Fluids
  • Post-Trip Inspection - Damage Reporting, Fuel Usage, Fault Reporting
  • Preventive Maintenance - Service Schedules, Tyre Rotation
  • Engine Servicing, Reflector Triangles (60m ahead/behind)
  • First Aid Kit, Fire Extinguisher, Tool Box Requirements
10
🔵 PROFESSIONAL DRIVER BEHAVIOUR
  • Discipline on Road - No Alcohol, No Phone Distraction
  • Patience in Traffic, No Road Rage (never retaliate)
  • Ethics & Responsibility - Respect for Pedestrians & Passengers
  • Road Courtesy, Fatigue Management, Rest Requirements
  • 8-Hour Driving Limit (Traffic Act Section 66A)

🏁 FINAL ASSESSMENT (NTSA STYLE)

Drivers are evaluated through comprehensive testing methods

📝 Multiple Choice
50 questions, 60 minutes, 80% pass mark
🚦 Road Signs
Identify and interpret all regulatory, warning, information signs
🎯 Scenario-Based
Real-world driving situations and hazard perception
⚖️ Safety Judgment
Decision-making and risk assessment

Minimum competency required for: Private drivers | Boda boda riders | PSV drivers | Fleet operators
