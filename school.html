👪 Parent & Child Safety Tips

👧 Teach Your Child

• Wait 3 giant steps back from the road
• Never run to catch the school bus/van
• Board one at a time, use handrail
• Stay seated during the entire journey

👩 For Parents

• Ensure child wears helmet if on boda
• Verify the school transport is NTSA licensed
• Report unsafe driving to school/NTSA
• Teach child emergency contact numbers

🚸 Crossing the Road

• Always use zebra crossing when available
• Stop, Look, Listen before crossing
• Make eye contact with driver
• Never cross between parked vehicles

✅ Daily Pre-Trip Checklist (School Driver)

🔧 Vehicle Inspection

✓ Tyres & brakes
✓ Lights & indicators
✓ Seatbelts all functional
✓ Emergency equipment (fire extinguisher, first aid, reflectors)
✓ Clean windshield & mirrors

📋 Documentation

✓ Valid PSV license
✓ School transport permit
✓ Insurance certificate
✓ Driver license with appropriate class
✓ Route plan & schedule

👥 Child Protocol

✓ Headcount before departure and after arrival
✓ No child left unattended in vehicle
✓ Drop only at designated stops
✓ Emergency contact list onboard

🆘 Emergency Evacuation Procedures

🚸 Evacuation Steps

1. Remain calm - children follow driver's lead
2. Use front door first if safe
3. Emergency exits/windows as backup
4. Assemble children at safe distance (50m from vehicle)
5. Headcount again
6. Call emergency services (999)

🔥 Fire Emergency

• Use fire extinguisher immediately
• Evacuate all children first (DO NOT save belongings)
• Move children upwind from smoke
• Call fire brigade
