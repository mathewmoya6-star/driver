📘 PSV Driver Modules

12 Comprehensive Modules covering all aspects of professional PSV driving for Categories B3, D1, D2, D3

01
📘 MODULE 1: PSV INTRODUCTION
  • Definition of PSV (Public Service Vehicle)
  • PSV categories (B3, D1, D2, D3)
  • Role of PSV driver in public service
  • Professional driver identity and responsibility
  • Difference between private and professional driving
02
⚖️ MODULE 2: LEGAL & NTSA FRAMEWORK
  • NTSA Act No. 33 of 2012 - Powers and functions
  • Traffic Act Cap 403 - Offences and penalties
  • Insurance requirements (Third Party Cap 413)
  • PSV licensing rules and PSV badge requirements
  • Vehicle inspection compliance (NTSA annual inspection)
  • Professional Driver's Licence (PDL) requirements
03
🧑‍🤝‍🧑 MODULE 3: CUSTOMER SERVICE
  • Core Principle: A PSV driver is a public representative of safety and service quality
  • Service Standards: Respect all passengers equally, no abusive language, calm professional communication
  • Passenger Care: Assist elderly, help persons with disabilities, ensure safe boarding/alighting
  • Conflict Handling: Stay calm, avoid physical confrontation, de-escalate professionally, report serious incidents
  • Clear route and fare explanation to all passengers
04
🧼 MODULE 4: GROOMING & PERSONAL PRESENTATION ⭐
  • Personal Hygiene: Clean body, deodorant, clean hands/nails, brushed teeth/fresh breath
  • Uniform & Clothing: Clean ironed clothing, no torn/dirty clothes, proper shoes (no slippers), smart casual or uniform
  • Hair & Beard: Neat well-kept hair, trimmed clean beard, no extreme hairstyles
  • Driver Appearance: No excessive tattoos showing, no offensive slogans, no messy appearance
  • Vehicle Presentation: Clean driver seat, no litter in cabin, organized dashboard, clean windshield
05
🚦 MODULE 5: PASSENGER SAFETY
  • Boarding procedures - safe and orderly
  • Seat discipline - all passengers seated
  • Overcrowding prevention - no standing passengers
  • Emergency exit awareness and location
  • Seatbelt enforcement for all passengers
  • Special seating for elderly, pregnant, and disabled
06
🛡️ MODULE 6: DEFENSIVE DRIVING
  • 3-Second Rule (following distance in normal conditions)
  • 4-Second Rule (adverse weather)
  • Hazard awareness and anticipation
  • Speed control - obey posted limits (PSV max 80km/h highway)
  • Weather driving techniques (rain, fog, night)
  • MSM technique (Mirror, Signal, Manoeuvre)
07
⏱️ MODULE 7: FATIGUE MANAGEMENT
  • 8-hour driving guideline (Traffic Act Section 66A)
  • Rest requirements - 30 min break after 4 hours
  • No intoxicated driving (0.00% BAC for PSV)
  • Fitness declaration before each shift
  • Consequences of driving while tired
  • Daily driving log requirements
08
🔧 MODULE 8: VEHICLE INSPECTION
  • Brakes - check for wear and response
  • Tyres - pressure, tread depth (1.6mm minimum)
  • Lights - headlights, indicators, brake lights
  • Mirrors - all present and adjusted
  • Horn - functional
  • Fuel system - no leaks, sufficient fuel
  • Emergency equipment - first aid kit, fire extinguisher, triangles
09
🚨 MODULE 9: EMERGENCY RESPONSE
  • Accidents - secure scene, call 999
  • Fire - evacuate passengers, use extinguisher
  • Medical emergencies - Dr. A.B.C, first aid
  • Breakdowns - reflector triangles (50m ahead/behind)
  • Security threats - anti-carjacking measures
  • Passenger evacuation procedures
10
🧳 MODULE 10: ROUTE & LUGGAGE CONTROL
  • Approved routes only - no short cuts
  • No overloading - passenger count within legal limit
  • Safe luggage handling - secure storage
  • Axle load compliance (EAC Act 2013)
  • Cargo securement - prevent load shift
  • Luggage weight distribution
11
📋 MODULE 11: ACCIDENT REPORTING
  • Stop immediately at accident scene
  • Assist passengers (evacuate if danger)
  • Notify police (999/112)
  • Insurance reporting within 24 hours
  • Documentation - photos, witness names, number plates
  • Report to employer immediately
12
⚖️ MODULE 12: NTSA COMPLIANCE
  • Valid PSV license (Professional Driver's Licence - PDL)
  • Insurance validity - third party minimum
  • Vehicle inspection compliance (annual NTSA inspection)
  • Zero reckless driving tolerance
  • PSV badge displayed at all times
  • Medical certificate validity (annually if over 70)
  • Police clearance certificate (for PSV operators)
