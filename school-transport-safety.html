🚸 School Transport Child Safety

NTSA-aligned professional training for safe transportation of school children, covering safety procedures, emergency response, and legal compliance.

NTSA APPROVED SAFETY COURSE

🎯 Course Purpose

This course trains drivers, conductors, and transport managers on how to safely transport school children with strict safety procedures and full legal compliance.

👥 Target Users

  • School Bus Drivers
  • Van & Matatu School Transport Drivers
  • School Matrons / Conductors
  • Transport Managers
  • Fleet Safety Officers

📘 Core Training Modules

Child Responsibility

Legal duty of care, child accountability, supervision rules.

Pre-Trip Inspection

Vehicle checks, seatbelt checks, emergency equipment.

Boarding Safety

Safe boarding, supervised entry, safe drop-off zones.

On-Route Safety

Speed control, behavior management, distraction prevention.

Emergency Response

Fire, accident, medical emergencies, evacuation steps.

Post-Trip Checks

Child counting, vehicle walk-through, reporting system.

🚨 Emergency Protocol

STOP VEHICLE → ACCOUNT FOR CHILDREN → EVACUATE IF NECESSARY → CALL EMERGENCY SERVICES
  • Never leave children unattended
  • Always confirm passenger count
  • Use rear exit first during evacuation

  • 30–50 Question Written Test
  • Minimum Pass Mark: 80%
