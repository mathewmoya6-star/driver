👥 Passenger Safety Tips

📌 Mounting/Dismounting

Wait for motorcycle to stop completely. Mount from left side. Hold rider's shoulders, not waist.

📌 Riding Position

Sit straight, keep feet on pegs, hold grab rail or rider's waist. Do not lean opposite direction.

📌 Luggage

Small items only. Use bungee cords. Never carry large loads that affect balance.

🌙 Night Riding Safety

💡 Lights & Reflectors

Ensure headlight, taillight, and reflectors working. Wear reflective jacket at all times.

🎯 Reduced Speed

Night reaction time slower. Reduce speed by 20-30% compared to day.

👀 Visibility

Assume you are invisible to other drivers. Position for maximum visibility.
