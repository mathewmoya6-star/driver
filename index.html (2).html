About DriverPrep Kenya

DriverPrep Kenya is the country's most comprehensive online platform for NTSA driving licence test preparation. We combine Kenya Highway Code content, interactive road sign revision, timed mock exams, and personal progress tracking — all designed around the actual NTSA test format.

Whether you're a first-time applicant or refreshing your skills, our platform gives you the confidence and knowledge to pass first time. Accessible on any device, anytime.
